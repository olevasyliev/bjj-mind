# BJJ Mind — Character Prompts for Nano Banana

Style reference for all characters:
- Cute, rounded, chibi-style (like the Gi Ghost mascot already in the project)
- Simple shapes, expressive eyes, minimal detail
- Consistent with the app's light/friendly tone
- Format: transparent PNG, facing slightly right (3/4 view)

---

## 1. Gi Ghost (Main Mascot)

The app's primary character. Already exists as `figma-screens/image 1.png` — use as style anchor for all others.

**Prompt:**
> A cute chibi ghost character wearing a white BJJ gi (kimono) with a black belt tied around the waist. The ghost has a round, soft body with no legs — it floats slightly. Big round black eyes with white shine dots, small oval nose, gentle neutral expression. The gi has faint collar stitching lines. Transparent background. Flat cartoon style, soft cel-shading.

**Variants needed:**
- Neutral (default) ← already exists
- Happy / celebrating (arms up, wide smile, star eyes)
- Sad / wrong answer (droopy eyes, small frown, head tilted down)
- Thinking (one arm up, finger near chin, tilted head)
- Focused / game mode (determined eyes, fists forward)

---

## 2. Marco (Senior Training Partner)

Blue/purple belt. Mid-20s vibe. Confident but warm. The player's guide and coach.

**Prompt:**
> A cute chibi male BJJ fighter character. Short dark hair, stubble, medium build. Wearing a blue gi with a purple belt. Big expressive eyes, slight smirk, friendly confident face. 3/4 view facing right. Transparent background. Rounded chibi proportions — large head, shorter body. Flat cartoon style, soft cel-shading. Slight athletic build visible in the shoulders.

**Variants needed:**
- Default / talking
- Proud / thumbs up (after Belt Test pass)
- Giving tip (finger raised, eyebrow up)

## 3. Old Chen (White Belt, 67 years old)

Started BJJ after retirement. Calm, wise, unhurried. Never in a rush.

**Prompt:**
> A cute chibi elderly male BJJ character. White hair, kind wrinkles around the eyes, slight smile. Wearing a clean white gi with a white belt. Relaxed, calm posture. Big warm eyes, gentle expression. 3/4 view. Transparent background. Rounded chibi proportions. Flat cartoon style, soft cel-shading. He looks peaceful and content.

**Variants needed:**
- Default / talking calmly
- Giving etiquette tip (slight bow, eyes closed, respectful)

## 4. Kat (Rival — Same Level, Always Ahead)

Silent, technically clean. The player chases her through all of white belt. No personality shown — just presence and skill.

**Prompt:**
> A cute chibi female BJJ fighter character. Short sharp hair (dark, undercut style), focused intense eyes, neutral expression — not unfriendly, just serious. Wearing a white gi with a white belt. Athletic compact build. 3/4 view facing slightly left (she's the opponent). Transparent background. Rounded chibi proportions. Flat cartoon style, soft cel-shading.

**Variants needed:**
- Default / neutral stance
- Victory pose (arms crossed, slight nod)

## 5. Rex (Training Buddy, Same Level as Player)

Big, good-natured, white belt just like the player. Makes the same mistakes. Learning together.

**Prompt:**
> A cute chibi large male BJJ character. Big round cheerful face, slightly chubby build (but strong), spiky hair, wide happy eyes, big open grin. Wearing a white gi with a white belt that looks slightly too small. 3/4 view. Transparent background. Rounded chibi proportions — even bigger head than others. Flat cartoon style, soft cel-shading. He radiates warmth and goofiness.

**Variants needed:**
- Default / grinning
- Confused / scratching head (after wrong answer)
- Excited / pointing (during tutorial)
