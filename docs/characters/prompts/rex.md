## ⚠️ EXPORT — READ BEFORE DRAWING

**The background must be 100% transparent (alpha channel). NO background. NO color. NO checkerboard.**

The grey/white checkerboard you see in your app is just your software showing "nothing is here." It is NOT part of the image. Do NOT paint it. Do NOT fill it. Do NOT flatten the canvas.

**Procreate:** Share → PNG → make sure the background layer is hidden (eye icon off) before exporting.
**Photoshop / Illustrator:** Delete or hide the background layer. Export As → PNG. Check "Transparency" is on.

If the exported PNG has a white or grey background when placed on a dark color — the export was done wrong. The character should float with no background on any color.

## The Character

Rexford Williams — goes by Rex, and if you call him Rexford, he'll pretend he doesn't hear you. 26, American (Georgia originally), in Barcelona two years, working remotely as a developer. His roommate signed him up for BJJ as a birthday joke. Rex showed up to that first class noticeably larger than most people in the room, fully expecting his size to handle it.

It did not handle it.

A 140-pound woman submitted him in 45 seconds. He paid for a year upfront that same day.

Rex is the training buddy — the one who texts you at 6pm "bro you training tonight?" even when he's tired. He makes the same mistakes the player makes, sometimes worse, but he's joyfully aware of his own limitations in a way that makes it easier to accept your own. Still sometimes tries to muscle out of a submission instead of tapping. The shoulder still hurts.

He celebrates the player's wins more than his own. When you earn a stripe, Rex acts like he earned it.

**Personality:** Loud enthusiasm, short attention span for theory, huge heart. Gets frustrated with himself, never with others. Self-deprecating humor as a defense mechanism. Just genuinely happy to be there.

**App role:** Tutorial companion (intro screens, aha moment). Occasional coach moment as a "wrong answer exemplar" — the question shows what Rex did and asks what went wrong. Appears in session summary celebrating with you.

## Visual Style (applies to all BJJ Mind characters)

- **Style:** Chibi / rounded cartoon. Large head ≈55% of total height, small body, stubby limbs.
- **Line weight:** Clean, consistent 3–4px black outline. No sketchy lines. No line weight variation.
- **Shading:** Flat cel-shading — one highlight pass, one shadow pass. No gradients except gi fabric folds.
- **Eyes:** Large, round, expressive. White sclera, black pupil, white shine dot in the upper-left of each eye.
- **Format:** Transparent PNG, 800×800px, character centered with 10% breathing room on all sides.
- **Orientation:** Slight 3/4 turn, facing right.
- **App theme color:** #7C3AED (purple)
- **Style anchor:** Gi Ghost neutral (`image 1.png`) sets the overall art language. Rex should feel like he exists in the same world.

## Rex — Visual Spec

**Body:**
- **The largest build in the entire character set** — wide, round, stocky.
- Strong, not flabby. But big.
- **Chibi proportions pushed even further** than the other characters — head is disproportionately large even by chibi standards.
- The gi visibly stretches across the shoulders — it's one size too small.

**Face:**
- Spiky messy warm medium-brown hair going in multiple directions — dressed fast, didn't check a mirror
- Big round cheeks
- Large round eyes — warm and slightly dopey-expressive in the best possible way
- **Wide genuine open smile as the default expression**
- **One tooth slightly more prominent** in the smile — not a gap, just one tooth that's a little bigger. Gives him character.
- Very expressive eyebrows — his whole face moves

**Gi:**
- White (#FAFAFA)
- **White belt — tied slightly unevenly.** One end is longer than the other. This is intentional.
- Gi visibly stretched across the shoulders (suggests one size too small)
- Gi collar slightly rumpled — like he's been in class a few minutes already

**Signature color:** Warm amber (#F59E0B) — his energy.

## Deliverables: 3 PNG files

### 1. Default / Grinning
- Wide open grin, eyes slightly squinted from the smile
- Arms relaxed at sides
- Warm, relaxed, happy to be alive

### 2. Confused / Scratching head
- Eyebrows raised and drawn together in a confused V-shape
- Mouth slightly open — like he's about to ask a question
- One hand raised to the back of his head, scratching
- Eyes looking sideways and slightly up — thinking, but slowly

### 3. Excited / Pointing
- One arm extended forward, pointing (at something off-screen or at the player)
- Eyes wide open, mouth in a big surprised grin
- Other arm slightly raised for balance
- The energy of someone who just understood something and needs everyone to know right now
