# Old Chen — Character Prompt for Nano Banana

---

## ⚠️ EXPORT — READ BEFORE DRAWING

**The background must be 100% transparent (alpha channel). NO background. NO color. NO checkerboard.**

The grey/white checkerboard you see in your app is just your software showing "nothing is here." It is NOT part of the image. Do NOT paint it. Do NOT fill it. Do NOT flatten the canvas.

**Procreate:** Share → PNG → make sure the background layer is hidden (eye icon off) before exporting.
**Photoshop / Illustrator:** Delete or hide the background layer. Export As → PNG. Check "Transparency" is on.

If the exported PNG has a white or grey background when placed on a dark color — the export was done wrong. The character should float with no background on any color.

## The Character

Chen Weiming, 67. Retired after 40 years running a dry-cleaning business in Barcelona's Raval neighborhood. Bored within a week. His grandson dragged him to a free trial BJJ class as a joke. Chen submitted the grandson in week 4.

He has a white belt because nobody gave him the test yet. He's never asked for it.

He doesn't train to compete. BJJ is the first physical practice he's ever done that requires real thinking, not strength or youth. He finds this philosophically interesting. He trains at the pace of someone who has decided there's no rush.

**Personality:** Still, observant, unhurried. Zero ego. Says it once, perfectly, and doesn't repeat himself.

**App role:** Occasional alternative coach voice — specifically for timing, control, and base concepts. His moments feel quieter and more philosophical than Marco's.

---

## Visual Style (applies to all BJJ Mind characters)

- **Style:** Chibi / rounded cartoon. Large head ≈55% of total height, small body, stubby limbs.
- **Line weight:** Clean, consistent 3–4px black outline. No sketchy lines. No line weight variation.
- **Shading:** Flat cel-shading — one highlight pass, one shadow pass. No gradients except gi fabric folds.
- **Eyes:** Large, round, expressive. White sclera, black pupil, white shine dot in the upper-left of each eye.
- **Format:** Transparent PNG, 800×800px, character centered with 10% breathing room on all sides.
- **Orientation:** Slight 3/4 turn, facing right.
- **App theme color:** #7C3AED (purple)
- **Style anchor:** Gi Ghost neutral (`image 1.png`) sets the overall art language. Old Chen should feel like he exists in the same world.

## Old Chen — Visual Spec

**Body:**
- Slightly smaller and more compact than Marco — not imposing.
- Relaxed posture — hands loosely behind back or lightly crossed. Never tense.

**Face:**
- White hair, short, neatly kept
- Clean-shaven — no beard or stubble
- Warm dark eyes, naturally slightly narrowed
- **Kind wrinkles around the eyes** — crow's feet, slight forehead lines. These must read as gentle, kind wrinkles, not harsh or severe ones.
- Default expression: **barely-there permanent closed-mouth smile** — corners of mouth just slightly up at all times. The face of someone who has seen many things and found most of them quietly amusing.

**Gi:**
- Immaculately clean white (#FAFAFA, slightly warmer than Gi Ghost to distinguish them), fabric shadow (#E2E5EB)
- **White belt** (#E2E5EB, subtle shadow #BBBFC7) — same rank as the player
- Gi is perfectly pressed and tidy. No wear marks anywhere. This man irons his gi.

**Signature palette:** Warm grey-white — calm, settled, nothing to prove.
