# Marco — Character Prompt for Nano Banana

---

## ⚠️ EXPORT — READ BEFORE DRAWING

**The background must be 100% transparent (alpha channel). NO background. NO color. NO checkerboard.**

The grey/white checkerboard you see in your app is just your software showing "nothing is here." It is NOT part of the image. Do NOT paint it. Do NOT fill it. Do NOT flatten the canvas.

**Procreate:** Share → PNG → make sure the background layer is hidden (eye icon off) before exporting.
**Photoshop / Illustrator:** Delete or hide the background layer. Export As → PNG. Check "Transparency" is on.

If the exported PNG has a white or grey background when placed on a dark color — the export was done wrong. The character should float with no background on any color.

## The Character

Marco Ferreira, 29. Born in Porto Alegre, raised in Barcelona. His grandfather immigrated from Brazil in the 80s and opened a gym Marco grew up in. Trains 5 days a week, coaches white belts on Tuesday and Thursday evenings. Purple belt, training like he's chasing brown. He will probably never stop chasing.

He's not soft with the user. He doesn't baby them. He points out the mistake, explains why it matters, then moves on. He treats you like someone who can handle the truth — which is the highest compliment he knows how to give.

**Personality:** Direct, warm, slightly competitive even when coaching. Explodes with joy when a student finally clicks on a concept. Short memory for mistakes — corrects, forgets, moves on.

**App role:** Post-session coach insights, belt test feedback, mid-session coach moment cards.

## Visual Style (applies to all BJJ Mind characters)

- **Style:** Chibi / rounded cartoon. Large head ≈55% of total height, small body, stubby limbs.
- **Line weight:** Clean, consistent 3–4px black outline. No sketchy lines. No line weight variation.
- **Shading:** Flat cel-shading — one highlight pass, one shadow pass. No gradients except gi fabric folds.
- **Eyes:** Large, round, expressive. White sclera, black pupil, white shine dot in the upper-left of each eye.
- **Format:** Transparent PNG, 800×800px, character centered with 10% breathing room on all sides.
- **Orientation:** Slight 3/4 turn, facing right.
- **App theme color:** #7C3AED (purple)
- **Style anchor:** Gi Ghost neutral (`image 1.png`) sets the overall art language. Marco should feel like he exists in the same world.

## Marco — Visual Spec

**Body:**
- Chibi proportions with a **slight athletic build visible in the shoulders** — wider than Gi Ghost.
- Stands planted and confident. Not tense, not hunched — relaxed but ready.
- Big head, short body, stubby limbs.

**Face:**
- Short, slightly messy dark brown hair
- Subtle jaw stubble — shadow only, 2–3 days growth, not a full beard
- Warm brown eyes, slight natural squint (he spends a lot of time thinking)
- Default expression: **calm half-smile** — not grinning, not serious. Just present and confident.

**Gi:**
- Color: Blue (#2563EB), fabric shadow (#1D4ED8), white highlights
- **Purple belt** (#7C3AED) with a visible knot — this is a key visual indicator
- Gi collar slightly settled and open — this gi has been rolled in
- Faint wear marks on the lapels (barely visible — adds character)

**Signature color:** Blue (#2563EB)
