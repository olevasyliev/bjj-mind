## ⚠️ EXPORT — READ BEFORE DRAWING

**The background must be 100% transparent (alpha channel). NO background. NO color. NO checkerboard.**

The grey/white checkerboard you see in your app is just your software showing "nothing is here." It is NOT part of the image. Do NOT paint it. Do NOT fill it. Do NOT flatten the canvas.

**Procreate:** Share → PNG → make sure the background layer is hidden (eye icon off) before exporting.
**Photoshop / Illustrator:** Delete or hide the background layer. Export As → PNG. Check "Transparency" is on.

If the exported PNG has a white or grey background when placed on a dark color — the export was done wrong. The character should float with no background on any color.

## The Character

Gi Ghost is the main mascot of the BJJ Mind app. Nobody knows exactly what he is. He appeared on the mat one day — friendly, in a white gi with a black belt — and nobody questioned it. The working theory: a BJJ practitioner so absorbed in the sport that when his body stopped, his mind stayed on the mat. He doesn't remember his name. The black belt he remembers — it cost him everything, and it's the one thing that followed him.

He's the user's constant companion in the app. Celebrates right answers, deflates on wrong ones. Not a teacher — more like a senior training partner who knows everything and never taps.

**Personality:** Endless optimism, but with a history behind it. Veteran enthusiasm, not childlike. Knows everything, but doesn't impose authority — just there.

---

## Visual Style (applies to all BJJ Mind characters)

- **Style:** Chibi / rounded cartoon. Large head ≈55% of total height, small body, stubby limbs.
- **Line weight:** Clean, consistent 3–4px black outline. No sketchy lines. No line weight variation.
- **Shading:** Flat cel-shading — one highlight pass, one shadow pass. No gradients except gi fabric folds.
- **Eyes:** Large, round, expressive. White sclera, black pupil, white shine dot in the upper-left of each eye.
- **Format:** Transparent PNG, 800×800px, character centered with 10% breathing room on all sides.
- **Orientation:** Slight 3/4 turn, facing right.
- **App theme color:** #7C3AED (purple)

## Gi Ghost — Visual Spec

**Body:**
- Round, soft oval body. No legs — floats approximately 15px above ground level.
- Arms: short, stubby, expressive — can hold different positions.
- The gi fabric has a slight translucency hint — he's a ghost. The material looks like very sheer cotton with a faint glow, not fully opaque.

**Face — IMPORTANT: not childlike, authoritative:**
- Large round head, but the **lower portion is slightly squarer** — not chubby baby cheeks.
- Eyebrows: **thick, set lower and closer to the eyes** — the look of someone who's "seen everything, knows everything." Slight resting heaviness, not anger — wisdom.
- Eyes: large, but **set slightly closer to center** — not baby proportions.
- Gaze: **calm and direct**, not naive.
- Mouth at rest: **a barely-there closed smile**, corners of mouth just slightly up. "I know everything, but I'll speak when it matters."
- Small scar above the left eyebrow — a hint at history. Barely noticeable, not dramatic.

**Gi:**
- Color: Pure white (#FFFFFF), shadow on fabric folds (#E8EAF0)
- V-neck collar, slight lapel visible
- Faint weave texture lines on the chest panel
- **Black belt** (#1a1a1a, highlight #3a3a3a) — tied correctly and neatly with a clean knot. This is his defining visual element.

**Aura:**
- Soft lilac glow beneath the body (#C4B5FD) — subtle suggestion of levitation, not overpowering.

**Style anchor:** The existing `image 1.png` is the Gi Ghost neutral reference. Match this energy across all expressions.

## Deliverables: 5 PNG files

### 1. Neutral (base — main style reference)
- Calm direct gaze, barely-there closed smile
- Arms relaxed at sides or lightly folded
- This is the anchor for the entire project

### 2. Happy / Celebrating
- Eyes: crescent arcs (^_^ shape), thick and expressive
- Mouth: wide open grin, small teeth visible
- Arms: both raised above head, slight left-right tilt (happy wiggle energy)
- Aura: brighter than usual
- Optional: 2–3 small ★ floating around him

### 3. Sad / Wrong answer
- Eyes: heavy drooping lids, small pupils looking downward
- Mouth: downward curve, small and tight
- Head: tilted slightly forward and down
- Arms: hanging limp at sides, lifeless
- Body: slightly smaller / "deflated" compared to neutral
- Aura: barely visible, very dim

### 4. Thinking / Focused
- One eye slightly narrowed, both eyes looking up-right
- Mouth: small, pursed — concentration face
- One arm raised, tiny ghost finger near the side of his head (not chin — no chin)
- Head tilted slightly to the right

### 5. Determined / Game mode
- Eyes: sharp, narrowed — "I mean business." Small pupils, intense stare.
- Mouth: straight line, slightly set
- Arms: both small fists raised forward and slightly up (fight stance energy)
- Body: leaning slightly forward
- Aura: slightly brighter, pulsing
