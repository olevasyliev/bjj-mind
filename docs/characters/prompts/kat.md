## ⚠️ EXPORT — READ BEFORE DRAWING

**The background must be 100% transparent (alpha channel). NO background. NO color. NO checkerboard.**

The grey/white checkerboard you see in your app is just your software showing "nothing is here." It is NOT part of the image. Do NOT paint it. Do NOT fill it. Do NOT flatten the canvas.

**Procreate:** Share → PNG → make sure the background layer is hidden (eye icon off) before exporting.
**Photoshop / Illustrator:** Delete or hide the background layer. Export As → PNG. Check "Transparency" is on.

If the exported PNG has a white or grey background when placed on a dark color — the export was done wrong. The character should float with no background on any color.

## The Character

Katherine Santos, 22. Half-Japanese, half-Brazilian. Grew up in Osaka, moved to Barcelona two years ago for university. Started BJJ eight months ago — a friend bet she wouldn't last a month.

She lasted. She hasn't missed a class since.

Not naturally gifted — methodical. Films herself after every class and watches the footage. Keeps a notebook. When taught a technique, she immediately asks "what breaks this?" — not to argue, but because she wants to know the counter before she gets caught by it.

She's technically ahead of where most people are at eight months. Not because of talent — because of attention. She pays more attention than anyone in the room.

She doesn't talk much at training. Not cold or unfriendly — just in a different mode on the mat.

**Personality:** Warm and likeable off the mat, quietly fierce on it. Competitive, but not mean about it. Genuinely happy for others when they improve. Has a bright smile that shows up when she wins — and she wins a lot. The kind of rival you root for even when she's beating you.

**App role:** The benchmark rival. The user chases Kat through all of white belt — she's always slightly ahead. Primary character on the vs Kat match screen, appears near the top of the league leaderboard, and is the final opponent in some tournament runs.

## Visual Style (applies to all BJJ Mind characters)

- **Style:** Chibi / rounded cartoon. Large head ≈55% of total height, small body, stubby limbs.
- **Line weight:** Clean, consistent 3–4px black outline. No sketchy lines. No line weight variation.
- **Shading:** Flat cel-shading — one highlight pass, one shadow pass. No gradients except gi fabric folds.
- **Eyes:** Large, round, expressive. White sclera, black pupil, white shine dot in the upper-left of each eye.
- **Format:** Transparent PNG, 800×800px, character centered with 10% breathing room on all sides.
- **App theme color:** #7C3AED (purple)
- **Style anchor:** Gi Ghost neutral (`image 1.png`) sets the overall art language. Kat should feel like she exists in the same world.

## Kat — Visual Spec

**⚠️ IMPORTANT: Kat faces LEFT — toward the viewer/player. 3/4 turn facing left. All other characters face right.**

**She is the only female character in the set — she should read as clearly and adorably feminine.**

**Body:**
- Slim, petite build — lighter and smaller than Marco
- Soft posture, not stiff or military. She's ready, but there's warmth to how she carries herself.
- Slightly smaller overall scale than the male characters

**Face:**
- **Cute, feminine chibi face** — softer cheeks, smaller nose, large warm eyes
- Dark hair, shoulder-length or pulled into a small ponytail with a few loose strands framing the face
- **Default expression: a small, slightly competitive smile** — friendly but confident. Like she's already one step ahead and she knows it, but she's not unkind about it.
- Large expressive dark eyes with long lashes — warm and attentive
- Overall vibe: the girl who's nicer than you expected and still beats you

**Gi:**
- Clean white (#FAFAFA), fabric shadow (#E2E5EB)
- **White belt** — same rank as the player
- **Small rectangular competition patches on the left shoulder** — simple color squares that suggest she's already registered for a tournament. This is a key detail that sets her apart from other white belt characters.
- Gi fits neatly — clean and well-kept, like she takes care of her gear

**Signature palette:** Soft white + warm accents — approachable, not cold.
